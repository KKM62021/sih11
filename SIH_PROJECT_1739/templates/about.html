About Us

Solar Scope is dedicated to advancing the understanding and implementation of solar energy solutions. Our mission is to provide accurate assessments of Building Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) potential using advanced 3D city modeling techniques.

We leverage satellite data from sources like Cartosat-1 and Cartosat-2/3 to develop detailed Level of Detail 1 (LoD-1) models. Our interactive application aims to simulate building shadows and calculate sunlight exposure, thereby helping policy-makers, solar energy providers, architects, and citizens make informed decisions regarding solar energy deployment.

Join us in our journey towards a sustainable future through the power of solar energy!
